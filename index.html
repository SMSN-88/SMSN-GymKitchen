About Us

We've beem in the fitness industry for 8 years now.

Welcome to SMSN's Gym Kitchen, we've been the go to kitchen for all your pre & post workout meals around the Dartford area and have just recently started doing Meal Plans for those of you who are too busy meal prep. Our Meal Plans are packaged as a 5 day Meal Plan, 2 meals per day for 5 days. We cater to all dietry requirements (vegan, Gluten free, Diary free, Nut Allergies). So why not pop in or give us a call and place that pre or post workout order today. Place a Meal Plan order before 2pm and pick up the same day.
